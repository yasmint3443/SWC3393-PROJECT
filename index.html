About

Voting is a method for a group, such as a meeting or an electorate, in
order to make a collective decision or express an opinion usually following discussions, debates or election
campaigns. Democracies elect holders of high office by voting. Residents of a place represented by an
elected official are called "constituents", and those constituents who cast a ballot for their chosen
candidate are called "voters". There are different systems for collecting votes, but while many of the
systems used in decision-making can also be used as electoral systems, any which cater for proportional
representation can only be used in elections.
