The election of the MPP in open mode is an openly contested MPP election where candidates are not contested according to a specific MPP position or exco that will be determined by the election committee

The candidate who won the highest number of votes in the election will be shortlisted to occupy the main positions of MPP which are president, deputy president, secretary and treasurer. The determination of the main position of MPP is entitled to be considered by the election committee based on the credibility or certain tendencies of the candidate. While the determination of positions for the other MPP exco is based on mutual agreement among the competing candidates.

**each voter can choose only 5 candidates
